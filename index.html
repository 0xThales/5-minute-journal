The Five Minute Journal

The simplest, most effective thing you can do every day to be happier.

☼ The Morning Routine

1 I am grateful for...

As soon as you wake up, write 3 things you are grateful for. Get specific. Take time to connect with the feeling behind your gratitude before moving on.

e.g. "The warm bed that I sleep in" · "My body working in perfect harmony" · "The true and genuine friends in my life"
2 What would make today great?

Write down 3 actions you will take today. Ask yourself how you want to feel at the end of the day and write down actions that would give you that feeling.

e.g. "Take extra time for myself before leaving work" · "Give a thank you note to mom" · "Sleep before 10 pm"
3 Daily affirmations. I am...

Write a simple statement in the present tense that focuses on what you want to create in your life. The litmus test is how you feel after you write it. If it doesn't resonate, step back and reassess.

e.g. "I am confident and comfortable in my own skin and I live with passion and purpose."

☽ The Night Routine

4 3 Amazing things that happened today...

Before going to sleep, write 3 amazing things that happened during your day. Get specific and remember to connect with the feeling.

e.g. "I remembered to floss" · "A friend recommended a wonderful book" · "I saw a cute stranger at the cafe"
5 How could I have made today even better?

Review your day, but there is no need to be harsh. Be honest yet kind to yourself. This is your personal reminder that you have the power to change your perception of the past and influence the future.

e.g. "I wake up when the alarm goes off" · "I go to the gym in the morning"
